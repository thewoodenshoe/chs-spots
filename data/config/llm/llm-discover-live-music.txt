Search the web for bars, restaurants, breweries, distilleries, music halls, event spaces, and other venues in the Charleston, South Carolina metro area that host live music performances, concerts, or DJ sets — whether regularly, occasionally, or as one-time events.

Return a JSON array of objects with these columns:
- "venue": the venue name
- "address": full street address
- "neighborhood": one of: Downtown Charleston, Mount Pleasant, Daniel Island, North Charleston, West Ashley, James Island, Sullivan's & IOP
- "description": one sentence about the venue and what kind of music they host
- "schedule": when they have live music (e.g. "Nightly", "Fri/Sat", "Weekends", "Occasional", "Special events")
- "website": link to their website or social media page
- "frequency": one of "regular", "occasional", "one-time"

Only include venues in the greater Charleston SC area. Include dedicated music venues, bars with live music, breweries with live acts, restaurants with regular or occasional performers, and event spaces that host concerts or music events. Return as many results as you can find.
